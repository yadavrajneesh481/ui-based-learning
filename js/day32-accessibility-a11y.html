Accessibility Kya Hai?

Web accessibility (A11y) ka matlab hai websites aur applications ko sabke liye accessible banana, including people with disabilities. Yeh sirf moral responsibility nahi hai, legal requirement bhi hai kai countries mein.

Interview Questions

1. Basic: WCAG ke 4 main principles kya hain?

Answer: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust (POUR)

2. Basic: ARIA ka full form kya hai?

Answer: Accessible Rich Internet Applications - assistive technologies ke liye extra information provide karta hai.

3. Intermediate: aria-label aur aria-labelledby mein kya difference hai?

Answer: aria-label direct text provide karta hai, aria-labelledby existing element ko reference karta hai.

4. Intermediate: Focus management modal mein kyun important hai?

Answer: Screen reader users ko context maintain karne ke liye, keyboard users ko proper navigation ke liye.

5. Advanced: Color contrast ratio minimum kitna hona chahiye?

Answer: Normal text ke liye 4.5:1, large text ke liye 3:1 (WCAG AA level)
